The Hyperloop is a new, futuristic mode of transportation conceptualized by entrepreneur and Tesla Motors CEO, Elon Musk. It is a high speed ground transportation system that relies on pressurized pods or capsules travelling inside a reduced-pressure tube. It is both cheaper and greener. And it is no longer just conceptual. Construction of a full scale prototype is already in progress.

Based on the "Hyperloop (Alpha)" white-paper , SpaceX is now organizing the Hyperloop pod competition to design the best pods.
